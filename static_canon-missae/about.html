FUNDING:

The project Liturgica Poloniae: Catalogue of Polish Liturgical Manuscripts. Part 1: Codices Copied until ca. 1300 is funded by the Polish state budget within the program of the Ministry of Science and Higher Education (Poland), entitled National Program for the Development of the Humanities, project number NPRH/DN/SP/495426/2021/10, the total value of the project: 1,457,924.00 PLN, PI: Paweł Figurski, PhD.
